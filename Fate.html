  • Average or Fair Physique gives you a 3-point physical stress box.
  • Good or Great Physique gives you 3- and 4-point physical stress boxes.
  • Superb+ Physique gives you 3- and 4-point physical stress boxes and an additional mild consequence slot.
  • Average or Fair Will gives you a 3-point mental stress box.
  • Good or Great Will gives you 3- and 4-point mental stress boxes.
  • Superb+ Will gives you 3- and 4-point mental stress boxes and an additional mild consequence slot.

Race Creation Worksheet

INTRO

We as humans are the most adaptable, versatile, and social species on this planet. We use tools to achieve things other species—probably smarter, faster, and more resilient than us could not. We use speech to coordinate and plan activities that not even the most strategic pack hunting predators could match. We use speech to teach future generations, thus eliminating the tedious 'trial by error' and significantly reducing adaptation times. And we use those abilities and more to dominate our environment making us the dominate species on this planet we call Earth.

Dominate Species Criteria (playable race):

Adaptable, ability to communicate, social, some measure of reasoning, ability to reproduce, and the ability or tools to change the environment to fit their needs and further the species.

NOTE: The better the species fits the planet in which it inhabits the more believable it will be. Try to make reasons for their existence and the way they look, act, etc. plausible in some way.
